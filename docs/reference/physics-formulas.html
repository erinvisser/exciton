Key Physics Formulas

All equations from the TALYS 1.96 manual Section 13, implemented in the exciton code.

Particle-Hole State Density

state_density.cpp:17 — Eq. 13.4

\[ \omega(p_\pi, h_\pi, p_\nu, h_\nu, E_x) = \frac{g_p^{n_\pi}\, g_n^{n_\nu}} {p_\pi!\, h_\pi!\, p_\nu!\, h_\nu!\, (n-1)!}\, (E_{\text{avail}})^{n-1}\, f_{\text{FW}}(p, h, U, V) \]

where \(n_\pi = p_\pi + h_\pi\), \(n_\nu = p_\nu + h_\nu\), \(n = n_\pi + n_\nu\), \(p = p_\pi + p_\nu\), \(h = h_\pi + h_\nu\), and \(E_{\text{avail}} = U - \Delta_{\text{Pauli}}\).

Pauli Correction

preeq_common.cpp:61 — Eq. 13.8

\[ A(p_\pi, h_\pi, p_\nu, h_\nu) = \frac{\max(p_\pi, h_\pi)^2}{g_p} + \frac{\max(p_\nu, h_\nu)^2}{g_n} - \frac{p_\pi^2 + h_\pi^2 + p_\pi + h_\pi}{4 g_p} - \frac{p_\nu^2 + h_\nu^2 + p_\nu + h_\nu}{4 g_n} \]

Finite Well Correction

preeq_common.cpp:71 — Eq. 13.10

\[ f_{\text{FW}}(p, h, U, V) = 1 + \sum_{i=1}^{h} (-1)^i \binom{h}{i} \left(\frac{U - iV}{U}\right)^{n-1} \Theta(U - iV) \]

Pairing Correction

preeq_common.cpp:98 — Eq. 13.5

\[ f_u(E_x/\Delta, n/n_{\text{crit}}) = \begin{cases} \Delta & E_x/\Delta < 0.716 + 2.44\,(n/n_{\text{crit}})^{2.17} \\[4pt] \Delta - \Delta\Bigl[0.996 - 1.76\,(n/n_{\text{crit}})^{1.6}\,(E_x/\Delta)^{-0.68}\Bigr]^2 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \]

Pairing Energy

preeq_common.cpp:122 — Eq. 13.7

\[ \Delta(A, \chi) = \chi \cdot \frac{12}{\sqrt{A}}, \qquad \chi = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{odd-odd} \\ 1 & \text{odd-A} \\ 2 & \text{even-even} \end{cases} \]

Critical Exciton Number

preeq_common.cpp:129 — Eq. 13.6

\[ n_{\text{crit}} = 2 g T_{\text{crit}} \ln 2, \qquad T_{\text{crit}} = \frac{2}{3.5} \sqrt{\frac{\Delta}{0.25 g}} \]

Single-Particle State Densities

preeq_common.cpp:6,12 — Eq. 13.9

\[ g_p = \frac{Z}{15}, \qquad g_n = \frac{N}{15} \]

Potential Well Depth

preeq_common.cpp:36 — Eq. 13.11

\[ V_{\text{well}} = \begin{cases} 12 + 26\,\dfrac{E_p^4}{E_p^4 + (250/A^{1/3})^4} & \text{neutron, }h=1 \\[6pt] 22 + 16\,\dfrac{E_p^4}{E_p^4 + (450/A^{1/3})^4} & \text{proton, }h=1 \\[6pt] 38 & h > 1\text{ or other projectiles} \end{cases} \]

Matrix Element (Kalbach-Cline)

transition_rates.cpp:34 — Eq. 13.26

Channel Matrix Elements

transition_rates.cpp:48 — Eq. 13.24

\[ M^2_{ab} = R_{ab} \cdot M^2 \]

Analytical Transition Rates

transition_rates.cpp:86,354 — Eq. 13.27

\[ \lambda_{\pi^+} = \frac{2\pi}{\hbar}\, \frac{g_p^2\, E_{\text{next}}^{n+1}\, f_{\text{FW}}(p+1,h+1,U,V)} {2n(n+1)\, E_{\text{curr}}^{n-1}} \bigl[n_\pi g_p M^2_{\pi\pi} + 2 n_\nu g_n M^2_{\pi\nu}\bigr] \]

Volume-Averaged Imaginary Potential

transition_rates.cpp:13 — Eqs. 13.29–13.30

\[ W(r) = W_v f_v(r) - 4 a_d W_d \frac{df_s}{dr}, \qquad \langle W \rangle = \frac{\int_0^\infty W(r) f_v(r) r^2 dr}{\int_0^\infty f_v(r) r^2 dr} \]

Collision Rate (OMP Kernel)

\[ \lambda_{\text{collision}} = \frac{2}{\hbar} \cdot \text{wompfac} \cdot \langle W \rangle \]

Wompfac Values

\[ \begin{aligned} \text{R}_{\pi\nu} &= R_{\pi\nu} / R_{\pi\pi} \\ \text{wompfac}_{\text{same}} &= M^2_c \cdot 0.55 / (1 + 2\text{R}_{\pi\nu}) \\ \text{wompfac}_{\text{cross}} &= M^2_c \cdot 0.55 \cdot 2\text{R}_{\pi\nu} / (1 + 2\text{R}_{\pi\nu}) \\ \text{wompfac}_{\text{conv}} &= \tfrac12(\text{wompfac}_{\text{same}} + \text{wompfac}_{\text{cross}}) \end{aligned} \]

Spin Distribution

preeq_common.cpp:148 — Eqs. 13.59, 13.61

\[ R_n(J) = \frac{2J+1}{\sqrt{\pi}\, k^{3/2}\, \sigma^3} \exp\!\left[-\frac{(J+\tfrac12)^2}{k\,\sigma^2}\right], \quad \sigma^2 = 0.24\, n\, A^{2/3}, \quad k = \begin{cases}2 & \text{TALYS compat} \\ n & \text{default}\end{cases} \]
